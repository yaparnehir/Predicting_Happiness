Our Goal

We are Group 4:Error 404, we undertook the task of trying to understan the influential factors impacting the happiness of a country while determing the accuracy of such factors and claims.The factors in question are: LOGGED GDP PER CAPITA, SOCIAL SUPPORT, HEALTHY LIFE EXPECTANCY, FREEDOM TO MAKE LIFE CHOICES, GENEROSITY, PERCEPTIONS OF CORRUPTION, INDEX OF INSTITUTIONAL TRUST, GINI COEFFICIENT OF INCOME(leves of income inequality), and UNEMP_SCORE(Unemployment rating at given time).

Challenges Faced

As we progressed carrying out ETL process for the data we realized our original large data had shrunk(in number of dimensions) due to the elimination of unneccessary/noisy data. Although the data being used was relevant and impacted "Happiness Score" we realized the dilema, information on how the scores were assigned was excluded from the source as well as, the mathematical computations and formulas to derive these results had been made exclusive/confidential. Our effort to find more relevant data that could impact a country/countries happiness became a more arduuos task than we realized since we had to ensure the integrity and accuracy of the data being merged.
